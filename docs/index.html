SPO-14582: Data Analysis

Author

Maik Bieleke, PhD

Overview

SPO-14582: Data Analysis is a seminar offered in the M.Sc. Sport Science for Health at the University of Konstanz. It provides an introduction to data analysis using the statistical programming software R. Students will learn the basics of R programming and how to use R for common data analysis tasks.

Teaching Team

Dr. Maik Bieleke is senior researcher and data scientist at the department of sport science at the University of Konstanz. His research focus is on sport psychology, with a special emphasis on motivation, effort, and performance in both laboratory and real-life settings. He has 10+ years of experience in working with R for data analysis and teaching R to students and researchers.
